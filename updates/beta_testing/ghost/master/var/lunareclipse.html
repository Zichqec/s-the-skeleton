When Is the Next Lunar Eclipse?

By Konstantin Bikos

There are at least 2 lunar eclipses each year. Find out when the next one is and if you can see it from your location.

The next lunar eclipse will be on July 5, 2020.

July 27/28, 2018 total lunar eclipse: User images

This eclipse will be visible in Pittsburgh. It will begin at 11:07:23 pm local time when the Earth's shadow will begin covering the lunar surface. The eclipse will be over at 1:52:21 am.

Check out the local weather forecast to find out if you will have clear skies to view the eclipse!
