A simple balloon to go along with Ukatranslator. The balloons are slightly transparent, which may cause the text to look slightly weird on dark backgrounds. I'm hoping one day there'll be better support for semi-transparent balloons.

Comes in 3 sizes.

Made by Zichqec https://zichqec.github.io/s-the-skeleton/
