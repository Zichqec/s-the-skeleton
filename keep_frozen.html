KEEP FROZEN


Download the latest .nar file here!

KEEP FROZEN is a ghost with multiple ways to play! Keep your ice cubes frozen and get your lifetime stats as high as possible, or melt them mercilessly to collect the items inside! The power is in your hands!

Originally made for Ghost Masquerade 5. This ghost is a collaboration with GallaTheGalla!
