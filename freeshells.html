Skeleton 00

Download here

A simple skeleton freeshell! This skeleton generally sports a grumpy or angry look. Multiple expressions and arm positions are available through intervals, to allow you to customize poses on the fly. You can also easily customize the colors to anything you like by simply changing the colors of a few files in any art program!
