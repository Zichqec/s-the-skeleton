Hydrate


Download the latest .nar file here!

Hydrate is a collab made with Zdzisiu from the Ukagaka Dream Team Discord server! It reminds you to drink water at a customizable interval, any amount of minutes up to 5 hours! ...And that's about it! This was made in about 3 hours, so please let me know if there are any bugs.
