Gallery 512


Download the latest .nar file here!

Gallery 512 is a ghost where you get to go to work and deal with your exceedingly normal coworker, and all of your lovely customers! Choose from a variety of canvas styles, paintings, and frames, in order to prepare your gallery for the lil guys that want to be your patrons!

Once you're all set, open the gallery and watch the lil guys wobwobwob on in! Maybe be careful what you let them see, though; some of them have bad opinions and you HAVE been warned!

Originally made for Ghost Jam 2023, in just 72 hours. This ghost is a collaboration with GallaTheGalla and Kat!
