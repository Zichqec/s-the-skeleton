Paper


Download the latest .nar file here!

Paper is a ghost by myself, Galla, and The Gibbous! A simple piece of paper to pester you with minigames. Wait, did I say pester..?

Paper was made as a part of Ghost Jam 2022, and was made within just 24 hours! It is almost entirely comprised of pre-existing minigames, but it does have a few thousand words that it can choose from dynamically based on the time of year/month/day/etc.
