Cibus


Download her latest .nar file here!

Cibus is a shell for the ghost FLELE! She's an OC of mine, not attached to any fandoms. She has a few dressup items, and will move her arms and make different expressions while singing. She also comes with a small bit of custom dialogue, if you choose to install it.



Not sure what FLELE is?

FLELE is a ghost that’s an MP3 player, basically! There are lots of different shells you can download for it.

To use FLELE, you’ll need to download the ghost here. It’ll be in Japanese, but thankfully, Zarla has created an English patch for it! I’ve included a custom version of that patch in Cibus’s files, you can find instructions for it in her readme.

Once you have FLELE running, you can drop Cibus’s .nar file on the character that pops up and it should switch to her automatically when it finishes installing. Drag and drop some music onto her and double click her face to make it play!

Here’s the full list of her hotkeys:
Face - Play
Shoulders - Stop
Dress (Belly and down) - Menu (This can be right clicked to bring up playlist options, too!)
Right foot - Song Bar
Left foot - Clear Playlist
Top of her head - Pet
